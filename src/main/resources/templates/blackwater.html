Blackwater aquarium

What is a Blackwater Aquarium? A blackwater aquarium is a type of freshwater aquarium that is characterized by its dark, tea-colored water. The water color is achieved by adding natural tannins, which are released from materials such as driftwood, peat moss, almond leaves and other botanicals. This type of aquarium is popular among hobbyists who want to recreate the natural habitat of fish species found in the Amazon basin and other tropical regions.
